Our Story

DesiGrooves was born from a passion for music, culture, and the unique analog warmth of vinyl records. As lifelong fans of Punjabi music, we noticed a gap in the vinyl market for classic and contemporary Punjabi artists. From golden-age legends to modern-day icons, our mission is to make their music accessible in the most authentic form.

We believe that music should be experienced — not streamed in the background — but truly felt, held, and cherished. That’s what vinyl does. It revives the emotions and energy captured in every beat and lyric. Whether you grew up listening to Gurdas Maan or just discovered the Nooran Sisters, DesiGrooves brings you closer to your roots through rich, analog sound.

Thank you for supporting a small business with a big love for music. Every record we sell is carefully curated, tested, and shipped with care. Join the vinyl revolution — Desi style.
